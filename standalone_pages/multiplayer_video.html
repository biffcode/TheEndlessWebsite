Frequently Asked Questions

When will multiplayer be available? +
We're planning to launch the multiplayer feature in Q3 2025. Join our waitlist to be notified when it's available and get early access opportunities.
How many players can join a single adventure? +
Our current design supports 2-6 players in a single adventure session. This provides the optimal balance between collaborative storytelling and ensuring everyone has a meaningful role.
Will multiplayer require a premium subscription? +
Basic multiplayer features will be available to all users. Premium subscribers will have access to enhanced features like longer sessions, more adventure templates, and special character abilities.
Can we save our multiplayer stories? +
Yes! All multiplayer adventures are saved automatically. Players can revisit them, continue from where they left off, or export them as a narrative to share with others.
What devices will support multiplayer? +
Multiplayer will be supported on desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux) and tablet devices at launch. Mobile phone support will follow shortly after the initial release.
